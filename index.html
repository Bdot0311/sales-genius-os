OutReign — Sales Operations Software for Outbound B2B Teams

OutReign is sales operations software that helps B2B teams describe their ideal customer in plain English, review qualified prospects, enrich contact data, and launch personalized outreach faster from one workflow. A modern alternative to Apollo, Instantly, Outreach, and Salesloft built for founder-led sales ops.

Sales Operations Software, Without the Tool Sprawl

Most sales ops stacks bolt together a prospecting tool, an enrichment tool, a sequencer, and a CRM. OutReign collapses lead search, ICP scoring, SMTP-verified email enrichment, AI email drafting, and reply management into one sales operations platform — so revenue teams ship outbound in minutes, not weeks.

How It Works

  1. Describe your ideal customer — Enter the job titles, industries, company size, geography, or buying signals you care about in natural language.
  2. Review matched prospects — See ranked leads with useful company and contact context so you can prioritize faster.
  3. Launch outreach — Generate more relevant outbound messages and manage replies without bouncing between disconnected tools.

Key Features

Plain-English lead search

Frequently Asked Questions

What is OutReign?
OutReign is a B2B prospecting and outreach platform that helps teams go from ICP definition to live outreach faster.
Do I need boolean search skills to use it?
No. OutReign is built around plain-English targeting.
Can I try it before paying?
Yes. You can start free and upgrade when you are ready.
Who is OutReign best for?
It is strongest for founder-led sales teams, outbound agencies, SDR teams, and B2B operators running outbound motions.
What integrations are available?
OutReign currently supports Google Workspace and is expanding support for tools like Slack, HubSpot, Salesforce, Calendly, and Zapier.
Is OutReign a fit for every business?
No. It is generally not the right fit for e-commerce, B2C, or companies that do not run outbound.
